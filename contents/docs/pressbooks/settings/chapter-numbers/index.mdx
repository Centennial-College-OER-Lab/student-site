---
title: Remove part and chapter numbers from the beginning of each chapter
description: This section provides an overview of how to remove part and chapter numbers from each chapter.
---

Pressbooks, by default, adds part and chapter numbers to the beginning of each chapter heading.

![Autogenerated chapter numbers](/remove-chapter-numbers/chapter-numbers.png)

1. To remove chapter numbers, click "Theme Options" under the "Appearance" tab in the left-hand panel.
2. Click the "Global Options" tab at the top.
3. In the "Part and Chapter Numbers" section, do not select "Display part and chapter numbers", then scroll to the bottom and click the "Save Changes" button.
   ![Unselected 'part and chapter numbers'](/remove-chapter-numbers/remove-numbers.png)
