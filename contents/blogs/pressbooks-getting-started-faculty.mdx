## Introduction: Welcome to Pressbooks!

This guide will walk you through the registration process so you can start creating, reviewing, and adapting your Open Educational Resources (OER).

**Pressbooks** is a powerful platform that allows you to easily create and adapt textbooks, course materials, and more in a variety of formats. Our OER Lab team is here to support you throughout the process.

## Why Pressbooks?

-   **Create Accessible Content:** Pressbooks ensures your materials are accessible to all students.
-   **Adapt Existing Resources:** Easily remix and revise existing OER to fit your curriculum.
-   **Cost-Effective for Students:** Provide free learning materials, reducing the financial burden on students.
-   **Open Licensing:** Empower you to choose how your content is used and shared.
-   **Multiple Export Formats:** Export your work in formats suitable for print, web, and e-readers (PDF, EPUB, MOBI, etc.).

## Step-by-Step Registration

Follow these simple steps to register for Pressbooks:

### 1. Navigate to Pressbooks

Go to the Pressbooks website using this URL: [Pressbooks Website URL](https://ecampusontario.pressbooks.pub/) (`https://ecampusontario.pressbooks.pub/`)

### 2. Navigate to Login Page

Click "Sign In" on the top-right navigation bar.

![Pressbooks Select Centennial College Screenshot](/pressbooks-getting-started-faculty/header-sign-in.png)

### 3. Sign In with "Institutional Login"

On the sign in page, click the "Institutional Login" button. This will open a "Find your institution" popup. Enter "Centennial College" and select the **second option** (You'll likely see two options appear in the dropdown).

![Institutional Login Button](/pressbooks-getting-started-faculty/school-selection.png)

### 4. Centennial College SSO Login

You will be redirected to the Centennial College Single Sign-On (SSO) login page. This is the same login you use for other Centennial College applications. Enter your Centennial College username and password.

![Centennial College Single Sign-On](/pressbooks-getting-started-faculty/centennial-sso.png)

### 5. Access the Pressbooks Dashboard (Admin Button)

After successfully logging in via SSO, you might land on the Pressbooks homepage. Click on the "Admin" link in the top-right navigation bar.

![Pressbooks Homepage with Admin Button](/pressbooks-getting-started-faculty/header-admin.png)

### 6. Check for Book Invitations

On the Pressbooks dashboard, look for a section labeled "Book Invitations." This is where you'll find invitations to join your book(s). If you don't see any invitations, you may not have been invited to any books yet, or they may have expired. Email your contact at the OER Lab team for assistance.

![Pressbooks Dashboard with Book Invitations](/pressbooks-getting-started-faculty/book-invitations.png)

## Next Steps After Accepting Invitations

Now that you've logged in and accepted your book invitations, you can:

* **Explore List of Books**: Hover over "My Books" on the top-left corner of the navigation bar.
![Accessing Available Books](/pressbooks-getting-started-faculty/accessing-books.png)

*   **Explore the Book:** Click on the book title to access the OER you're contributing to.

*   **Edit and Contribute:** Depending on your role, you can edit chapters, add content, and collaborate with other authors. 

*   **Contact the OER Team:** We're here to help! Reach out to mindylee@centennialcollege.ca.

**Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)**

- **Having trouble loggin in?** Ensure you're selecting the **second** "Centennial College" option during institutional login.

- **Not seeing any book invitations?** Ensure that you have logged into the correct account via the Centennial College SSO (link/anchor to the step). If you are in the correct account but don't see the book invitation, reach out to Mindy Lee, OER Faculty Lead, at mindylee@centennialcollege.ca.

**Need Help?**

For other questions about Pressbooks, reach out to your contact at the OER Lab or the Faculty Lead at mindylee@centennialcollege.ca.
