The Centennial College OER lab team has developed a series of engaging H5P activities for the **Thanatology CETH 100** course! These activities are designed to enhance learning through interactive experiences, making complex concepts accessible and enjoyable.

Key Features of the H5P Activities:
- Interactive Learning: Students can engage directly with the material, allowing for deeper understanding.
- Creative Design: Each activity is thoughtfully crafted to be visually appealing and user-friendly.
- Collaborative Projects: These activities showcase the teamwork involved in their creation, reflecting the combined expertise of the OER lab team.
